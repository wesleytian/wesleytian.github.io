About

Wesley has a couple official roles, but in reality, those are just labels. Deep inside, he just wants to be happy and free, but he is under society’s spell to do well and get a good career. His goal is to break that spell eventually and truly disregard his own meta-judgment as well as others to live a fulfilling life that will benefit mankind.

Wesley lives by a few philosophies.

  1. It is the ultimate goal of mankind as a whole to explore the universe(s). Biologically speaking at least. I want to help achieve that goal by expending my resources on spacefaring.

  2. We only have one life, as far as we know it, so it’s best to enjoy all that life has to offer, regret nothing, and to try new things often. This is supported by the exploitation vs. exploration tradeoff.

  3. Never be afraid to be different, or to speak your thoughts. It’s people who are different that do great things. It is more valuable to know you’re wrong than to not know anything.
